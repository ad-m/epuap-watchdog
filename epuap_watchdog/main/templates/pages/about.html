What is the purpose of this site?

The website is currently designed to make it easier for you to find contact information for ePUAP offices.

What things I can get on the Internet with the authorities?

Few official matters in Poland are possible to settle on the Internet. Unfortunately, there is no universal right to deal electronically, and only certain procedures allow such an opportunity. I advise contacting a solicitor if you do not want to study the rules and case law for hours.

What is the data source for the page?

The website is designed to use only publicly available official information. A collection of address packs in ePUAP system, REGON database and TERC database has been used.

We lack the appropriate quality of geographic data. We hope that the relevant authorities will publish it soon.
